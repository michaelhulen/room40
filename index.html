About

Two friends. One mission.
Pack every room.

Emerging in 2025 from Tampa, FL, Room40 blends EDM and house with high-energy, hard-hitting influences into a genre-defying sound that's become a staple of the Tampa Bay nightlife scene.

What started as two friends experimenting behind the decks quickly turned into packed rooms, festival main stages, and residencies at some of the region's most recognized venues.

Their sound lives at the intersection of approachable, mainstream-friendly house and groove-driven energy — blending familiar tracks with fresh drops that keep crowds locked in from start to finish.
